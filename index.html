Questions people actually ask

Are these real items?

Every one. The photos are the official game renders, and the stock count is what's actually ready to trade. If it says ×2, there are two waiting for you.

How did you set the prices?

Each item is priced against what it's actually selling for on the big item shops, plus one dollar. NFL Universe items are priced off their in-game RAP against real-money sellers.

What if an item sells out?

Stock counts update as people buy. If something you paid for is gone by trade time, you pick a replacement or get that line refunded.

Is this affiliated with Roblox?

No. Randomblox is a fan-run shop and isn't connected to Roblox, Nikilis, Uplift Games or Voldex.
