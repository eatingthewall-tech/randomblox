Refunds & Delivery Policy

Delivery times

Most orders are delivered the same day. Your order enters a queue after payment; when you reach the front, a VIP server link unlocks and we complete the trade in-game. You can track your place any time from the "My order" button, and message us in the live chat if you need help.

Refunds

  • If we can't fulfill it: if an item is out of stock or we cannot deliver for any reason, you receive a full refund automatically.
  • After delivery: because these are virtual items handed over by in-game trade, an order is considered complete once the trade has been accepted, and completed orders are generally non-refundable.
  • Something went wrong? If an item was wrong, missing, or not delivered, contact us within a reasonable time and we will fix it or refund you.

Refunds are returned to your original payment method through Stripe.

These are our standard terms — the shop owner may offer a refund at their discretion in other cases. Questions about a specific order are always welcome in the live chat.

Privacy Policy

We only collect what we need to deliver your order and support you.

What we collect

  • Your name, Roblox username and email — to identify your order, deliver your items, and send your receipt and updates.

  • Basic order details (items, price, order number).

Payments

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Your card, PayPal or Cash App details are entered on Stripe's secure page — we never see or store your full payment details.

How we use it

  • To fulfill and support your order. That's it.
  • We do not sell your personal information or share it with anyone except the payment processor needed to complete your purchase.

Storage on your device

The site uses your browser's local storage to remember your cart and your order's queue status. This stays on your device and is not used to track you across other websites.

Your choices
